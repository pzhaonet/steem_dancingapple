周日阳光明媚，而周一傍晚开始下雨。

画一幅画，像是一场静止的修行，窗外日出日落、晴雨更迭，这画面都是恬静的模样。

画工笔画或者写小楷，是漫长且缓慢的过程。

等水洇，等墨干。

低头静画闲窗前，蓝天清昼，又出去忙碌了一天归来，老位置，雨生过凉，慢慢涂，慢慢描……

不知不觉，就到了完成的时候。

After a beautiful and bright Sunday, a rainy Monday evening came.

Painting a picture is like a peaceful practice. 

Outside the window, it is sunrise and sunset, sunny and rainy. This picture is a quiet appearance.

Fine-brush painting or Calligraphy is a long and slow process.

Wait for spread of ink and then wait it dry.

Inside the window, keep painting peaceful with head down. Outside the window it is a blue sky. 

Came back after a busy day and sit at old place. Feeling little coldy after rain, but I paint slowly and slowly.

Unconsciously, it’s time to finish.

材料：

毛笔、宣纸、国画颜料、墨水、水、镇纸、印章

Material:

Writing brush, rice paper, Chinese painting pigment, ink, water, paperweight, seal
