Apple旅行小碎片#01 大鱼小鱼- apple's travel memento#01 Big Fish Small Fish

dancingapple

2018-08-10 01:59 GMT

原文链接: steemit, cnsteem, busy, chainbb, steemdb, steemd, busy, markdown

【好重一条鱼】

帕劳。

话说，举起这条大鱼的时候我内心飞过无数不可描述的弹幕。

重也就算了，手触摸的地方又滑又腻，左手从鱼鳃位置深入，那里有着许多参差不齐的牙齿，我必须小心翼翼用力，否则它会越咬越紧。

放下它的时候，我一度害怕自己的手抽不出来，因为被咬得很紧，动一下就痛。后来手抽出来了，但是上面却布满牙痕，有些还流血了。

好吧，事实证明，装x是要付出代价的。



【One heavy Fish】
Palau.
When I lifted the big fish, innumerable indescribable live subtitles flew inside my heart.
Although dead, the fish is not only heavy, but also slippery and greasy. My left hand is deep in the position of its gill. There are many uneven teeth. I have to be careful, or it would be more tight in my hand.
When I put it down, I was afraid I couldn’t pull my hand out because it was so tight. Then the hand was hurt and but it was covered with dental marks and some bleeding.
Well, as it turns out, to show off need to pay the price.
【好多鱼】

以前不大理解为啥会有人花重金打造水族箱鱼池啊什么的，直到我亲自下海喂鱼……额，这句话好像听着哪里不对劲，哈哈哈。好吧，我喂的鱼们都是吃素的。

我把吐司用力捏成一团，然后潜入水中，当我把手掌松开的时候，吐司碎末随水散开，疯狂的鱼儿们迅速冲过来狂吃。

超近距离看它们，那小表情真的滑稽又可爱，像一群贪吃的小朋友，这么有灵气的小家伙根本让我无法将它们和“食物”联系在一起。

额……虽然我依旧很爱吃鱼。



[A lot of fishes]
I didn’t quite understand why people spent a lot of money on aquarium fish ponds until I went down to feed the fish myself… Well, it sounds like something’s wrong with it. Ha-ha-ha. Well, all the fishes I feed were vegetarian.
I squeezed the toast into a mass and then dived into the water. When I was loosing the palm of my hand, the pieces of toast scattered with the water and the crazy fishes rushed over to eat it.
Looking at them at close distance, their expression is really funny and lovely, like a group of greedy little friends.I can’t link such smart little guys with “food”.
Although I still love to eat fish.
【阴森滴鲨鱼】

当我在沙滩狂奔或者凹造型拍照的时候，压根想不到不远处并不算深的海水里盘旋着鲨鱼们。

对，好多好多条鲨鱼。

浮潜在水中，像只海星一样舒展开，身体随海水涌动……

话说，假装自己不是人类就能在鲨鱼面前隐身吗？哈哈。至少它们确实不鸟我。


[A gloomy shark]
When I was running on the beach or taking pictures in concave shapes, I couldn’t imagine sharks hovering in the not-too-deep water not far away.
Yes, a lot of sharks.
Snorkeling in the water when the sharks were below, I stretched out like a starfish and the body surges with the sea.
In other words, can you pretend that you’re not human in order to hide from sharks? Ha-ha. At least they paid me no attention.
【人为刀俎我为鱼肉】

香港·长洲

从中环过去长洲很方便，直接刷八达通入闸登船，不用半个钟就到。

不知道为什么每次去长洲我都会有一种在澳门+鼓浪屿混合体的错觉。

长洲码头沿岸停靠了许多小渔船，岸边晾晒着鱼干虾干以及黄澄澄的金桔。

话说那金桔被晒得像晶莹剔透的琥珀，看得我垂涎三尺。



不过还是说回主角——一条被宰ing的大青斑。

作为吃瓜群众，作为折腾过类似这么大一条鱼的人……感叹档主当真练得一手好功夫。

一下子功夫，一条大鱼被处理得干净利落。

啊……我又很想喝鱼汤了。




[Man as one fish]
Cheung Chau, Hongkong
From Central to Cheung Chau, it is very convenient. It takes less than half an hour.
I don’t know why every time I go to Cheung Chau I have the illusion of a mixture of Macau and Gulangyu.
Many small fishing boats were docked along the coast of Cheung Chau Wharf, drying dried fish, shrimps and oranges.
The orange was sunburned like a crystal clear amber, which made me salivate.
But let’s go back to the protagonist, a big bass about one meter long.
As an onlooker, who has handled one fish of much smaller size. I expressed amazement and wonder at the fish seller, who really is really good at handling fish.
In a flash, a big fish was handled neatly.
Ah… I’d like to have fish soup again.
